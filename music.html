Music

I am a very keen musician for most of my life, and this is my main interest outside of electrical engineering and computer programming. I am fluent at piano and violin and have played in various concerts and competitions over the years (although not won much. Alas, that is why I changed career paths!). Recently, I have been increasingly interested in music composition, and I am in the process of writing a variety of tracks for a 2D SNES-style RPG I have in progress.

This page is intended to be a showcase of the pieces I have completed, as well as some in an advanced state of completion. If you enjoy listening to them even a fraction as much as I've enjoyed writing them, then I feel the whole thing will have been worth it.

Battle Theme

This is intended to be the main battle theme the RPG game. It is inspired by '80s Sci Fi themes and '90s video game music, with pulsating guitars and space-age FM synths. It is intended to be both intense, yet kinda catchy at the same time. I intend the mechanics of the game's battle system to be much like a 2D fighting game, so this intense, fast-paced theme reflects this.
