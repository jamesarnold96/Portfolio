Why NeZOOMi?

Most related products in the market focus on inspection rather than cleaning of underground pipes. Products such as EnviroSight Rovver X and SuperVision carry complex and expensive camera technology, which increases the size and complexity of the machine. Other, less intelligent products include manual drain snakes, which are inserted through plug holes. Others include drain cleaning machines, sink machines and drum machines. These devices perform designated actions and are rather inflexible.
