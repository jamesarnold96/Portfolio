About Us

Internet of Good Brains is an exciting new start-up providing innovative IOT solutions for sewage and underground drainage cleaning. Our smart products help to overcome the limitations of traditional cleaning solutions. Our four members are Electrical and Electronic Engineering students at Imperial College London, who are passionate about changing the world and making a difference through smart technology.
